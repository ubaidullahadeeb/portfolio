Privacy Policy – FIXNCENT

This Privacy Policy explains how FIXNCENT collects, uses, protects, and manages your personal information. By accessing or using the FIXNCENT website or mobile application (the “Platform”), you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy along with our User Agreement. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use the Platform.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

a) Information We Collect

When a user registers on the FIXNCENT Platform or books any of our services (Maintenance Services, Construction Services, Architect Services, Inspection Services, IT Networking, Waterproofing, Cleaning Services, AC Services, Electrician Services, Geyser Services, Plumber Services, Handyman Services, Carpenter Services, Painter Services, Pest Control, Home Appliances services), we collect the following:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Optional profile information

Users who only browse our website without signing up are not required to provide personal information.

b) Why We Use Your Data

  • Account creation and verification
  • Managing service bookings
  • Contacting you regarding your service orders
  • Sending notifications related to your account and service status

Your information enables us to ensure accurate service delivery within Lahore.

c) How to Request Data Deletion

Users may delete their account anytime through the “Delete Account” option available in the app’s account settings.

When an account is deleted, all personal information provided during signup or service bookings is removed from our system.

You may also request data deletion by emailing us at:
📩 support@fixo.com

d) Unlinking Social Logins (Google / Facebook)

If you signed up using social login, you can unlink your Google or Facebook account through the account settings. Once unlinked, all related profile data will be removed from your FIXO account.

e) Personal Data Removal Requests

  • Account settings
  • Email request (support@fixo.com)
  • Contact form on our website

All valid data deletion requests will be processed promptly.

1. Location Access for FIXO Partner App

The FIXNCENT Partner App may access background location solely to:

  • Provide nearest service requests
  • Improve order allocation accuracy
  • Enable timely arrival notifications for service providers

Location data is used only for performance optimization within Lahore and is not shared with third parties.

2. Cookies and Anonymous Identifiers

Our Platform may store cookies or anonymous identifiers on your device for login convenience, preferences saving, and improved user experience. Cookies help us recognize returning users and speed up the sign-in process.

3. Protecting Your Personal Information

FIXNCENT takes reasonable and modern security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. We recommend keeping your login details private.

4. Third Parties

FIXNCENT does not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third party.

5. Marketing & Communication

By registering on the FIXNCENT Platform, you may receive service updates, promotional offers, new service announcements, and important notifications.

You can opt out of promotional messages at any time, but administrative messages cannot be disabled unless you delete your account.

6. Administrative Messages

Account alerts, security messages, and service-related updates are essential and cannot be opted out of unless you permanently delete your FIXNCENT account.

7. Accessing, Editing, or Deleting Your Information

You may access and update your personal details anytime through account settings. You may also request deletion via:

📩 Email: support@fixncent.com

📞 Phone: +92324-4066289

Some data may remain temporarily stored due to backup systems but will not be actively used.

8. Privacy Concerns & Complaints

If you have any concerns or questions, please contact us through our website or email. All complaints are reviewed and resolved promptly.

FIXNCENT may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of major changes.

9. Service Delivery Policy

Our service professionals aim to reach your location in Lahore within the expected timeframe. Arrival times may vary due to traffic, distance, and service type.
